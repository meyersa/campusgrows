The Central Michigan University gardening RSO!

What we do

We started a growing area on campus, by the baseball fields, to allow students and residents to grow food despite not having the resources

Who we are

CampusGrows is a group that supports planting on campus and empowering students to be able to learn how to grow their own food

How you can get involved!

Simply show up at one of our information events and you can start planting in the garden too, anyone can do it!

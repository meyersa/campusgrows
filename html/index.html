The Central Michigan University gardening RSO!

What we do

We hold events around gardening and improving the campus garden, while spreading education about how to garden and the benefits of growing your own food.

Who we are

CampusGrows is a group that supports planting on campus and empowering students to be able to learn how to grow their own food

Giving back

We have a community garden section where people are invited to donate their time to help grow food for the community to be donated to the CMU Student Food Pantry

Get involved!

Our garden is located just west of lot 70 off of West Campus Drive by the baseball stadium. We rent 10ft by 10ft plots to any individual who is interested for $10, or you can contribute to the community garden any time!
